The Evolution of bicycle

In 1817 Baron von Drais invented a walking machine that would help him get around the royal gardens faster: tow same-size in-line wheels. The front one steerable, mounted in frame upon which you straddled. Teh device was propelled by pushing your feet against the ground. The device was propelled.Thus rolling yourself and the device forward in a sort of gliding walk.
